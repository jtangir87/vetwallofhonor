Wall of Honor

In honor of Veterans Day 2020, the Southeastern Pennsylvania Cold War Historical Society has created a Virtual Wall of Honor to commemorate and remember United States Military veterans who are current or former residents of southeastern Pennsylvania.

This project allows us to preserve the memories and share insights for each of the honorees’ military service in an accessible, virtual format.

Use the Name Search feature to find a name on the virtual wall, or browse the virtual wall below.

We are always looking for more information about those we honor. If you have information, photographs or wish to leave a remembrance for any of the individuals on our Virtual Wall of Honor, please reach out to us via our contact form
